# Employee Attrition & Retention Dashboard (Power BI)
A Power BI dashboard that analyzes **employee attrition, retention trends, and HR insights** using the IBM HR Analytics dataset. The report provides a clear view of **attrition rate, employees left, job satisfaction, income trends**, and breakdowns across demographic and job-related categories.

---
## Key Features

* Interactive slicers: Department, Job Role, Gender, Age Group, Marital Status, Education, Overtime
* KPIs: Total Employees, Employees Left, Attrition Rate, Avg Monthly Income, Avg Years at Company
* Visuals:

  * Attrition by Department
  * Attrition by Age Group
  * Total Employees by Job Role (Yes/No Attrition)
  * Attrition by Education Field
  * Job Satisfaction Gauge
  * Employees Left by Gender

---

* **Power BI Desktop**
